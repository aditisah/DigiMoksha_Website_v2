Welcome to the Three-Month UX Design Course, led by an industry veteran and tailored for non-designers already working in tech companies. In today's competitive landscape, user experience (UX) design has become a critical component for success in the digital world. This course is designed to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in UX design, even if you come from a non-design background

Led by our experienced industry veteran, who has a wealth of practical knowledge and a deep understanding of the UX design landscape, this course will provide you with a comprehensive and hands-on learning experience. Over the course of three months, we have carefully structured weekend classes that accommodate your busy schedule, allowing you to balance your work commitments while immersing yourself in the world of UX design.

Whether you are a developer, product manager, marketer, or any other professional working in the tech industry, this course will empower you to understand and apply UX design principles effectively. We believe that a diverse skill set enhances collaboration and drives innovation. By embracing UX design, you will gain a valuable perspective that will elevate your work, influence product decisions, and create delightful experiences for your users.

Throughout the course, you will delve into the key aspects of UX design, including user research, information architecture, interaction design, visual design, and usability testing. Our hands-on approach will ensure that you not only grasp the theory but also put it into practice through real-world projects and exercises. You will have the opportunity to collaborate with your peers, exchange insights, and receive personalized feedback from our industry veteran, enabling you to grow and refine your design skills.

Beyond technical skills, we will explore the importance of empathy, understanding users' needs, and fostering a user-centered mindset. By adopting these principles, you will gain a competitive edge in delivering products and services that truly resonate with your target audience.

Upon completion of the course, you will receive a certificate recognizing your achievement and newfound expertise in UX design. This credential will bolster your professional profile and open doors to exciting career opportunities in the field.
